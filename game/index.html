How to play 🌷

  1. Collect: tap a blooming flower to gather petals into the Fresh tray. Flowers re-bloom after a little while.
  2. Dry: tap a fresh petal to place it on the drying board. Wait for the ring to fill — then it's a dried petal.
  3. Craft: tap a dried petal to press it into a matching coloured spot on the pattern. Complete the picture to finish the craft and start a new one!

Tip: each craft tells you which colours it needs. Plan your picking!
